What is Organix?

Organix is your personal productivity space, designed to help you stay in control of your goals, ideas, and daily routines. More intuitive and flexible than traditional tools, Organix adapts to the way you live, so you can focus on what truly matters.

With Organix, you can:

  1. - Create and manage your own projects effortlessly
  2. - Organize tasks with customizable boards and cards
  3. - Track your habits and routines with multiple views
  4. - Visualize your progress at a glance
  5. - Keep everything synced across all your devices
